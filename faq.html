FAQ

CAN I CHANGE OR CANCEL MY ORDER AFTER CONFIRMED BOOKING?

If you want to change a reservation, we will help me with this as long as it is more than 48 hours before the original time. You can also transfer the booking to another name. Unfortunately, we do not accept cancellations, but you can change your booking up to one year in advance.

WHAT ARE THE OPENING HOURS?

Weekdays: 1600 - 2200. Saturday and Sunday: 0800 - 2200. Go to the booking pages and see which times are available for the offer you wish to use.

HOW OLD DO YOU HAVE TO BE?

Varm i Vollen has an age limit of 18. Children accompanied by adults must be at least 6 years old and must be able to swim. All use of the sauna and bathing is at your own risk.

WHAT HAPPENS UPON ARRIVAL?

On arrival at Varm, you will meet your sauna host, who will inform you about your stay and safety related to using the sauna boat.

DO YOU HAVE A CHANGING ROOM?

Our sauna has its own changing room and external hot shower.

DO YOU HAVE TOILETS?

Yes, as a guest at Varm, you have access to toilets at Vollen Marina.

WHAT ARE THE GENERAL GUIDELINES FOR THE USE OF HEAT AND SURROUNDING AREAS?

In order for everyone to have a good experience, we inform you that shouting and screaming, heavy drinking and nudity are not accepted. All use of the sauna and bathing is at your own risk.

WHAT ARE THE RULES FOR THE USE OF ALCOHOL IN YOUR HOUSE?

Varm i Vollen has no service on board, but you can take your desired drink/snack with you in your bag in the sauna when booking privately. We limit this to two units per person.

Guests in the area who are visibly intoxicated due to alcohol or other drugs will be rejected and the booking will be canceled without refund. We do not recommend alcohol combined with ice baths and saunas.

WHAT SHOULD I BRING?

Pack your own bag with swimwear, towel, drinking water, bag/bag for wet clothes.

SHOULD I AVOID WEARING JEWELERY?

Many types of jewelery quickly become very hot and uncomfortable against the skin, so it is wise to leave them at home.

CAN I BRING MY OWN FOOD/SNACKS?

You can take light snacks with you to enjoy in the changing room or out on deck. For hygienic reasons, food is not allowed inside the sauna.

CANCELLATION POLICY.

If you want to change a reservation, we will help me with this as long as it is more than 48 hours before the original time. You can also transfer the booking to another name. Unfortunately, we do not accept cancellations, but you can change your booking up to one year in advance.

SAFETY

During your stay at Varm, there will be a sauna attendant present, who will inform you of where to find a first aid kit, fire extinguisher and lifebuoy. The host has completed courses in life-saving first aid and can answer questions about the stay. You will find notices with fire instructions on the inner wall in the changing room.

We have a limit on consumption of up to two units of alcohol per day. guest on Warm. This has been set for the safety of the guests. People who show up visibly intoxicated will be expelled. The sauna host can expel intoxicated persons who pose a safety risk or who are otherwise a nuisance.

A FIRST AID KIT IS PLACED IN THE WARDROBE AREA.

Fire extinguisher is located to the left of the exit door.

All bookings for Drop In and Private bookings must be made here on our booking pages. You pay with VIPPS or Credit card. ​

We have a maximum limit for Private Sauna and Drop In of 8 pr people pr session. ​
